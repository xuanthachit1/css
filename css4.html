Rainbow lorakeets

The rainbow lorikeet is a medium-sized parrot. The head is deep blue with a greenish-yellow nuchal collar and the rest of the upper parts(wings, back and tail) are deep green. The chest is red with blue-black baring. The belly is deep green, and the things and rump are yellow with deep green barring. In flight a yellow wing-bar contrasts clearly with the red underwing coverts.

There is little to visually distinguish between the sexes; however, to a keen observer of their colouring and behaviour,their dimorphism id readily apparent.
